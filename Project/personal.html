A bit about me

Actually I have a very big family, I have four siblings. Two brothers and two sisters, I'm the second older. I came to Canada with my father.

My mother stayed in Brazil, I used to live with her before came to Canada. I had two cats and a dog. All of them had to stay too.

My siblings and I are very close, we normally hang out and do stuff together.

All my family loves movies and TV series, we always watch movies together, I love travel and meet different cultures, that's one of the reasons why I liked Montreal a lot.

I learned saxophone when I was 12, I love all kind of music, but mainly soundtracks. Any soundtrack! Movies, games, series.. there is no limits.

Before studying Programming, I graduated in Eletronic, as a technician. I also did four years of Pharmacy in UFF-Universidade Federal Fluminense, which is an university in Brazil.

I'm from Niteroi, Rio de Janeiro. I lived there 21 years of my life. It is a very beautiful place, however is dangerous. I've chosen some pictures from internet to show you my city.
